Bangle

Bangle is a completely free code editing and web hosting service, that allows people to make and host html, without having to set up a costly system.

Why choose Bangle?

Bangle has always been free, which makes it a great option for sharing a small project, or hosting a website.

Bangle's code editor lets you see your site in real time, which is helpful for debugging or getting a UI just right.

With one click of a button, you can share and control access to your source code, great for collaborators. Just invite someone to your project by typing in their email!
